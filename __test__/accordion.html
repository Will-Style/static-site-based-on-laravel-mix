[data-accordion]でwrapした要素内にある[data-accordion-trigger]をトリガーとし、[data-accordion-trigger]のaria-controls属性に指定したIDを付与した[data-accordion-content]要素を開閉します。
初期で開いたままにしておきたい場合はtriggerとcontentのどちらにも.c-accordion__activeを付与します。 

<div class="c-accordion" data-accordion>
    <button aria-controls="accordion-1" type="button" class="c-accordion__trigger c-accordion__active" data-accordion-trigger>Accordion01</button>
    <div id="accordion-1" data-accordion-content class="c-accordion__content c-accordion__active">
        <div class="c-accordion__inner">
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing ...
        </div>
    </div>
    <button aria-controls="accordion-2" type="button" class="c-accordion__trigger" data-accordion-trigger>Accordion01</button>
    <div id="accordion-2" data-accordion-content class="c-accordion__content">
        <div class="c-accordion__inner">
            Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing ...
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
